01

Overview

This project involved a louver-style thermal and mechanical system concept for a space mission context.

The page uses selected mechanism visuals, report figures, and PDFs to explain the design without publishing raw working files.

02

Problem

Thermal louver systems need controlled opening and closing behaviour while satisfying mechanical and thermal constraints.

The project required clear documentation of mechanism intent, thermal context, and design trade-offs.

03

My Role

  • Contributed to mechanical/thermal design documentation
  • Prepared public web visuals from organised images and reports
  • Kept large simulation videos out until compressed
04

Process

  1. Mechanism concept

    The work focused on a louver-style mechanism and how controlled motion could support a thermal-control function.

  2. Thermal and mechanical context

    Report and presentation figures were used to communicate the thermal-control problem and mechanism layout.

  3. Public page preparation

    Selected images and PDFs were included while raw working files and large media stayed outside the website.
